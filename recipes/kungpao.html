Kung Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken filled with bold flavors and mouth-watering spiciness

This asian delicacy is a tasty recipe that can be made pretty quickly, but still creates a wonderfully savory profile.

The spicy chile peppers compliment the more tame bell peppers, to create a dish that holds a daily roster in my diets line up.

Ingredients needed:

  • Boneless chicken (beef or pork works as well)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Your favorite Seasonings (I use Chicken bouillone and Costco steak seasoning)
  • Colored or regular green bell peppers
  • Celery
  • Spicy Habanero peppers and dried chile peppers (if spiciness is desired)
  • Minced Garlic
  • Ginger puree
  • Soy Sauce
  • Hoison Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Rice vinegar and Soy sauce

Steps to make the dish:

  1. Cook the chicken thoroughly using your preferred method. I usually put them in an instapot and let it pressure cook on the “Poultry” pre-setting, which is around 30 minutes with about 8 pounds. I will drizzle some oil on the meat and sprinkle seasoning as well for flavor before closing the lid.

  2. Set the meat aside, and fill the pan with a good amount of butter or Oil. Put half a spoonful of garlic, Along with about half a spoonful of ginger puree.

  3. Chop up the habanero peppers and dump all of it into the pot, along with the dried chile peppers. While peppers fry for a little, Chop up the bell peppers and celery. Put the vegetables into the pot as well.

  4. Pour the soy sauce, rice vinegar, oyster sauce, and hoison sauce all into the pot. Stir to mix.

  5. To add to the amount of sauce, pour in your desired amount of broth. If the sauce turns into a more soup-like texture, then make a roux with the cornstarch to thicken it up. I usually use cornstarch mixed with broth or butter and microwave in a covered bowl until it turns into a mashed potato consistency. Mix into the sauce.

  6. Finally, combine the chicken with the sauce. Make your favorite entree carbs to compliment the sauce, or eat straight like this if your particular about your diet like me!
