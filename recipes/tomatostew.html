Tomato Meat Stew

Hearty Beef and Tomato Stew packed with vegetables

This extremely quick to make but delicious recipe has only a few ingredients, but still creates a wonderfully savory profile.

The way the tomatoes, garlic, and vegetables all come together make this dish a go to in my diet.

Ingredients needed:

  • Beef (Chicken or pork works too)
  • Cooking Oil
  • 2-4 cans of Diced tomatoes
  • Your favorite Seasonings (I use Chicken bouillone and Costco steak seasoning)
  • Vegetables like onions, carrots, peppers, or corn
  • Minced Garlic
  • Ginger puree
  • Any broth

Steps to make the dish:

  1. Cook your chosen meat thoroughly using your preferred method. I usually put them in an instapot and let it pressure cook on the “meat stew” pre-setting, which is around 28 minutes with about 6 pounds. I will drizzle some oil on the meat and sprinkle seasoning as well for flavor before closing the lid.

  2. Set the meat aside, and fill the pan with a good amount of butter or Oil. Put half a spoonful of garlic, Along with about half a spoonful of ginger puree.

  3. After bringing heat to the pot, throw in the cans of tomatoes and stir.

  4. Chop up your vegetables and dump them into the pot along with your seasoning. Mix well.

  5. To add to the amount of sauce, pour in your desired amount of broth.

  6. Finally, combine the meat with the sauce. Make your favorite entree carbs to compliment the sauce, or eat straight like this if your particular about your diet like me!
